The Tigger Movie centers on Tigger, the enthusiastic and bouncy tiger from the Hundred Acre Wood, who longs to find others of his kind. Tigger loves boasting that the most wonderful thing about Tiggers is that he is the only one, but deep down he yearns for a family, specifically a Tigger family tree filled with other Tiggers just like him. He begins searching through an old book for clues about his family and becomes convinced that his Tigger family is out there somewhere and will come to visit him. He writes them a letter and eagerly waits for a response. When no reply arrives, his friends in the Hundred Acre Wood, including Pooh, Piglet, Roo, Eeyore, Kanga, Owl, and Rabbit, feel sorry for him and secretly write him a letter pretending to be his family and promising to visit. Tigger is overjoyed and prepares for their arrival. When the supposed family fails to show up, Tigger sets off into the snowy mountains alone to find them, ignoring warnings about dangerous winter weather. His worried friends go after him and a perilous avalanche threatens everyone. Roo, who has come to admire Tigger deeply throughout the film, rallies the others and they work together to rescue Tigger. Once safe, Tigger is heartbroken to learn the letter was fake, but his friends reveal that family does not have to share the same stripes. The friends he has known all along are his true family. Tigger embraces this truth and finds joy and belonging in the family that was always around him.
